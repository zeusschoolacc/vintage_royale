Josephine Elderfield

Vintage Royale CEO

Introducing the QUEEN of Vintage Royale, Josephine P. Elderfield. Founded in the midst of the pandemic, Vintage Royale is a small, online business handled solely by Josephine herself. Vintage Royale specializes in sourcing and distributing timeless, luxurious, and classic Royal Albert china, directly imported from the United Kingdom. So if you ever find yourself in the mood to add just a touch of elegance and charm to your life, Vintage Royale is the way to go. Whether it be for special occassions or for everyday indulgence, Vintage Royale offers the opportunity to elevate ordinary moments into extraordinary experiences, surrounded by the beauty and grace of vintage allure.
